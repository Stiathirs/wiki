[b:Combat] is an integral part of the game. To make progress, you will need to slay monsters that get in your way.

[#:Basics]

There are two things you must keep track of to survive combat:

  • Your Health
  • Their Health

[i:Yeah that's it, legit.]

During combat, you can take 3 different actions:

  • Attack
    • Attack the monster using your current weapon.
  • Inventory
    • Use an item to help you in combat.
  • Flee
    • Run away from combat.
      • This action ends your adventure immediately.
      • You lose 1/6 of your stash if you win initiative and immediately flee, otherwise it's 1/4.
[#:Initiative]

The one who goes first is determined by an Initiative roll.

  • You roll 1d20 + (Dexterity / 3) rounded down.
  • The opponent rolls 1d20 + KillStreak.
    • This means that enemies will begin to quickly outroll you.
[#:Damage]

Over the course of combat, damage will be inflicted to you and your opponent. who could've guessed?

  • You deal WeaponDie + (Strength / 2 - 1) damage rounded down.
  • The enemy deals 1d8 + KillStreak damage.

To max out the amount of damage you inflict, check out [[Weapons]], and check [[Armor]] to reduce the damage you take.
